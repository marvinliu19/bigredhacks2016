How it Works
Having information about the weather can help farmers optimize their planting and harvesting periods. While farmers in the developing world may not have access to standard means of obtaining weather updats, around 80% of them have a cell phone that can send and recieve sms text messages. Our service allow users to recieve timely sms updates on local weather conditions and crop information. By sending simple text messages to a number, they are able to recieve the information in a timely and inexpensive way.
Features
Commands:
subscribe < crop >: weekly update on price
local < crop >: get locale acerage of crop
price < crop >: get commodity price of crop
seven day forcast: get weather forecast for next 7 days
subscribe forecast: update on weather every 3 days

Potential features:
